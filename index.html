Key Features of our TSD Rally App

Roadbook Mode

With 3 different "Modes", 3pRally is the most adaptable and configurable TSD Rally App.

Use "Roadbook Mode" to configure and follow a Roadbook, while getting all the tips to win it ("Speed Up/Down" and "Time vs Ideal").

Advanced Mode

All the info you need at a glance, without having to configure a Roadbook in the app.

Current Speed, Average Speed, Partial Distance and Time.. and of course Total Distance and Time (Coordinates can be hidden if not needed).

Classic Mode

Here is our favorite one, "Classic Mode". The one we started with, more than 10 years ago!

"Classic Mode" focuses on one metric at the time (Time, Speed or Distance) and let you change it with just one simple tap on th‍e next metric you want to monitor. So simple!

Roadbook Design

Configure your Roadbook within the App and follow it in "Roadbook Mode".

Just type in two of the values  (and the type of stage between CAST, Time, Transfer and Pause) and the app will calculate all the others.

Additional Speed

For your Special Stage you need to reset the Average Speed without resetting the other Partial Counters?

Enable “Independent Partial Average Speed” in Settings and resetting the Partial Speed (green 3pRally icon) will not reset the Partial Counters.

Countdown

Set Auto-start (Settings) with the delay you need (30 or 60 seconds or configure a specific time).

With Auto-start on, the app can also count down (beep or voice) 5 or 10 secs before the starting time (very handy).

Calibration

Easily calibrate the app odometer against the Calibration Section.

The applied "% correction" will be always visible next to the distance (in a tiny green box).

Counters Adjustment

Need to manually change the distance (one-off) or want to permanently add a “correction factor” to the distances once started a route?

Just double tap on of the distance labels!

Reverse

Missed a turn or took the wrong exit at a roundabout?

Tap the Reverse Button and continue to drive until you are back on track! 

Night Mode for Low Light Conditions

Driving in the night?
‍
Enable “Dark Appearance” in settings and the app will change its color to be more readable in low light situation . 

Tracks

Using the Map View, to follow your progress on the map!

Voice Assistance for Safe Driving

To make sure you can focus on the right things, while driving... 3pRally can help you with keeping you up to date with your Current Speed, Average Speed and Partial Distance but also giving you Tips ("Roadbook Mode" only) about Speed and Time vs Ideal.

Flic

Do you want a handsfree navigation? Add the Flic Button!

What is Flic!?! Flic is a bluetooth smart button that can be used to  control the 3pRally app without touching your iPad/iPhone.

learn more about Flic

Export

Help
