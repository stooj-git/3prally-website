Privacy Policy

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application “3pRally” and “3pRally Pro” (“Applications” or “Application”) for mobile devices that was created by 3pMaster Ltd.

This Privacy Policy describes how and when 3pMaster Ltd collects, uses and shares your information when you use our Applications. 3pMaster Ltd receives your information through our website and our ”Applications". For example, you send us information when you contact us or use our app. When using any of our Applications you consent to the collection, transfer, manipulation, storage, disclosure and other uses of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Irrespective of which country you reside in or supply information from, you authorize 3pMaster Ltd to use your information in the United Kingdom and any other country where 3pMaster Ltd operates.

If you have any questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at “privacy [at] 3pmaster.com”.

User Provided Information

The Applications obtain the information you provide when you download and register the Applications. Registration with us is optional. However, please keep in mind that you may not be able to use some of the features offered by the Applications unless you register with us within the app.

When you register with us and use the Applications, you generally provide (a) your name and your email address and other registration information; (b) information you provide us when you contact us for help.

We may also use the information you provided us to contact your from time to time to provide you with important information, required notices and marketing promotions.

Automatically Collected Information

In addition, the Applications may collect certain information automatically and not associated to the user information, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browsers you use, and information about the way you use the Applications such as the distance and the time traveled, the area where the tracks was recorded (minimum and maximum coordinates).

All the automatically collected information are store anonymously and cannot be linked to the user information.

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

These Applications do not collect precise real time information about the location of your mobile device.

The information collected about your mobile device location are limited to the minimum and maximum coordinates of the area within you traveled during a track and cannot be linked to a specific user.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

Only aggregated, anonymized data is periodically transmitted to external services to help us improve the Applications and our service. We will share your information with third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy statement.

We may disclose User Provided and Automatically Collected Information:

  • as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process;

  • with our trusted services providers who work on our behalf, do not have an independent use of the information we disclose to them, and have agreed to adhere to the rules set forth in this privacy statement.
  • if 3pMaster Ltd is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, you will be notified via email and/or within our Applications and/or via a prominent notice on our Web site of any change in ownership or uses of this information, as well as any choices you may have regarding this information.

What are my opt-out rights?

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling the Application. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network. You can also request to opt-out via email, at “privacy [at] 3pmaster.com”.

We will retain User Provided data for as long as you use the Applications and for a reasonable time thereafter. If you’d like us to delete User Provided Data that you have provided via the Applications, please contact us at “privacy [at] 3pmaster.com” and we will respond in a reasonable time.
